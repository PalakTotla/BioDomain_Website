About Us!

Bioequipzon is a platform for researchers to access the specific equipment they need for their R&D work that is either available in the particular city or institute of their interest.

The main objective of this website is to provide support to the researchers by providing them with a list of instruments available in various institutes across the country.

    • Centrifuge

    A centrifuge is a device that uses centrifugal force to separate various components of a fluid. This is achieved by spinning the fluid at high speed within a container, thereby separating fluids of different densities or liquids from solids.

    Cell culture is the building block in life sciences research on all platforms; whether to produce recombinant proteins or virus particles or to make cell models.

    • Electrophoresis

    Electrophoresis is a laboratory technique used to separate DNA, RNA, or protein molecules based on their size and electrical charge. An electric current is used to move molecules.

    • Chromatography

    Chromatography is a process for separating components of a mixture. To get the process started, the mixture is dissolved in a substance called the mobile phase, which carries it through a second substance called the stationary phase.

    Polymerase chain reaction (PCR) is a common laboratory technique used to make many copies (millions or billions!) of a particular region of DNA. This DNA region can be anything the experimenter is interested in.
